Mole Removal Service

Moles can cause numerous and extensive damages to the properties they infest. Some of these hazards include an increased risk of personal injury due to uneven ground/burrows which can result in liability issues, the destruction of ornamental plants by diverting water-flow into the tracks of their underground feeding tunnels, and unsightly visual damage to the landscape.

Animal Damage Management provides multiple options for mole control. We can use rodenticide baits, live or kill traps and we can exclude them from your property using underground screening. Our prices will differ depending on the method chosen, the amount of activity present, and the size of the area to be controlled. We guarantee all mole work we perform. Please call us so we can setup an appointment for you to meet with one of our fully trained technicians or supervisors.

Comprehending Moles

Signs

Moles leave cone shaped mounds of dirt wherever they are creating a den or food storage area. Moles will create uplifted "tracks" where they are burrowing just under the ground level these are typically seen along sidewalks/buildings.

Our Solution To Your Problem

Animal Damage Management uses a variety of rodenticides, fumigants, trapping devices, CO2 machines, or rodenators to remove the problem. We take into consideration the location, ecosensitive areas, natural wildlife and cost into which method is chosen.

Quick Facts!

  • Mole Dangers: Moles should never harm a human directly. The damage from moles comes from mound systems becoming tripping hazards or slope failure.
  • Mole Food: Moles are carnivores. They will grubs and worms and use there tunnel systems to catch these animals.
  • Mole Habitat: Moles inhabit all of North and South America, Europe and Asia. Here in California we have a variety of species that live from Sierra Nevada Mountains to the San Diego Coast. Typically moles are found in low lying areas that retain moisture.
